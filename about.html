About

Hello! My name is Xiangyi Chen (陈相宜).

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Life Sciences and Technology at Tongji University in China. Before coming to Tongji University, I obtained my Bachelor’s degree from College of Informatics, Huazhong Agricultural University.

My current research focuses on utilizing single-cell sequencing technology to investigate the influence of genes and the environment on diabetes. Additionally, I'm exploring the application of deep learning to address complex biological questions.
